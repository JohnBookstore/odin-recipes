Casserole

A filling Southern style casserole

Ingredients

  • 1 package extra wide egg noodles
  • 1 pound hamburger meat
  • 8 ounce sour cream
  • 8 ounce cottage cheese
  • 8 to 12 ounces tomato sauce depending on taste
  • 2 cups shredded taco cheese

Directions

  1. Cook package of egg noodles per package directions
  2. Drain pasta and in large bowl mix together with sour cream and cottage cheese
  3. Fully cook hamburger meat and drain
  4. Return hamburger meat to stove and mix in tomato sauce
  5. Bring meat and sauce to a light simmer for 2 minutes
  6. In a casserole dish add a base layer of noodles followed by the meat and sauce and the remaining noodles on top
  7. Top with cheese
  8. Add dish to a preheated oven at 375 until cheese is melted
