Ramen Egg Recipe

Ramen eggs are a great addition to any asian dish or enjoyed on their own.

While easy to make this dish is hard to master but your patience will be rewarded

Ingredients

  • Four large eggs
  • 2 oz soy sauce
  • 2 oz mirin
  • 2 oz sake
  • 1 tsp white sugar
  • Ice for water bath

Directions

  1. Mix the soy sauce, mirin, sake and sugar together in a pot and bring to a boil
  2. Once boiling turn down heat to simmer for one minute
  3. Set aside to cool
  4. Bring a pot of water to a full roiling boil
  5. Add eggs to boiling water
  6. Turn down heat slightly to maintain slow boil as to not toss around eggs
  7. Boil eggs for 7 1/2 to 8 minutes from time first egg is added
  8. Make an ice bath to shock eggs
  9. Once time is up add eggs to the ice bath for 15 minutes
  10. Carefully peel eggs and add to ziplock bag with cooled sauce mixture
  11. Let eggs marinate for several hours or preferably overnight
  12. Slice egg in half and enjoy
